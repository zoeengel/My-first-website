PSYTRANCE & MUSIC FESTIVALS

PSYTRANCE

I really enjoy listening to the different genres of psytrance, some of the sub genres that fall under psytrance include dark prog, forest and goa trance. This music genres has little to no lyrics but it's the melodies that really speak to you, to your soul.

Just like with other music genres, theres be concerts where the artist perform and play their music live, in this case we call it a fesitval instead of a concert, where there'll be a line-up comprising of multiple Dj's, each with atleast a two hour set.
